Make the design more confident, not merely louder.

Use this skill when the interface works but feels safe, generic, too evenly weighted, or emotionally flat. If the user wants a full in-place upgrade, use `redesign-ui`. If they want a specific aesthetic lane, consider `editorial-minimal`, `premium-soft`, or `industrial-brutalist`.

## Diagnose Weakness First

Find which of these problems are making the work feel timid:

- typography with little voice
- equal-weight sections where nothing leads
- generic card rows or symmetric layouts
- weak focal point or no hero moment
- surfaces with no depth or structure
- no meaningful hover, active, or reveal states
- color used too cautiously to create hierarchy

Do not amplify everything. Pick `1-2` high-impact axes.

## Choose the Amplification Strategy

Common moves:

- stronger display typography with sharper hierarchy
- a more asymmetrical or editorial composition
- clearer scale contrast between hero, body, and metadata
- one more disciplined but more present accent color
- a more tactile interaction model
- a stronger image, figure, or structural framing device

## Amplify With Restraint

### Typography

- increase contrast between large and small text
- use tighter tracking and stronger leading control on headlines
- replace safe defaults with type that has a point of view
- preserve readable body copy widths

### Layout

- break generic equal-card rows
- create one dominant area instead of many medium-priority zones
- use whitespace structurally, not decoratively
- let asymmetry create tension only when the layout still collapses cleanly

### Surface & Color

- strengthen the neutral base and one accent instead of adding many colors
- use borders, frames, captions, or figure treatments to increase structure
- add depth through layering or tint, not generic glow

### Motion

- add purposeful hover, focus, active, and reveal behavior
- motion should make the interface feel alive, not busy

## Guardrails

- Do not turn every element into a statement piece.
- Do not use AI-cliche gradients, glass everywhere, or dark-mode “coolness” as a shortcut.
- Do not break readability to chase energy.
- Do not increase motion before hierarchy is fixed.
- Do not amplify a weak composition with decoration.

## Skill Routing

- `frontend-design` for major new UI work
- `redesign-ui` for upgrading an existing product in place
- `premium-soft` for polished premium warmth
- `industrial-brutalist` for aggressive structural impact
- `polish` after the stronger direction is established

Boldness succeeds when the product feels more intentional and more memorable, not simply more intense.
