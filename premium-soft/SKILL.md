Use this preset when the interface should feel polished, modern, and expensive without becoming cold, brutal, or theatrical.

## Core Mood

- soft neutrals or near-white backgrounds
- generous breathing room
- diffused depth instead of harsh contrast
- refined typography with a premium sans or gentle serif pairing
- subtle, haptic-feeling motion

## Layout

- prefer asymmetric but calm compositions
- use floating groupings, split layouts, or offset sections instead of rigid equal-card grids
- preserve wide margins and clear reading zones
- if using shells or framed panels, keep them light and structural rather than glossy

## Typography

- headlines should feel composed and premium, not shouty
- body copy should stay easy to read for long stretches
- metadata can use mono sparingly, but never as the dominant voice

## Surface Treatment

- use whisper borders, soft shadows, and controlled tinting
- nested surfaces are acceptable when they communicate physical layering clearly
- glass is allowed only in small, fixed, or clearly justified areas; never as a blanket treatment

## Motion

- smooth ease-out or spring motion with modest travel distance
- subtle hover tension and tactile pressed states
- no theatrical blur-heavy reveals

## Avoid

- generic luxury tropes like random blur, huge pills everywhere, and weak cream-on-beige contrast
- over-stacked cards and shells that waste space
- loud gradients or candy accents
- agency-style motion that draws attention away from content

Use this preset for interfaces that should feel calm, high-end, and human.
